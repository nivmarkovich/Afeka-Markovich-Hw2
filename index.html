Architecture

  1. Input: 28×28 grayscale (1 channel)

  3. Flatten feature maps
  4. Dense layer → softmax (3 classes)
  5. Loss: cross-entropy; optimization: gradient descent with manual backprop

Trained weights and biases are saved to localStorage (JSON) and loaded automatically on startup when the architecture matches.
